# Microeconomics: A Complete Open Textbook (English Summary)

**Original title:** *Microeconomia: Uma Abordagem Completa*
**Language:** Portuguese (Brazil)
**Author:** Darcio Genicolo-Martins, Assistant Professor of Economics at Insper
**License:** Open access

## About This Book

This is a comprehensive microeconomics textbook covering 27 chapters across consumer theory, game theory, firm theory, market structures, labor economics, intertemporal choice, information economics, externalities, health economics, environmental economics, digital economics, and experimental methods. It targets advanced undergraduates, master's students, and candidates for Brazil's ANPEC graduate economics entrance exam, bridging the gap between Varian and Mas-Colell with rigorous yet accessible treatment — and extensive Brazilian empirical content.

## Distinctive Features

- **Brazilian empirical content**: 75 "Box Brasil" sections with real data from IBGE, Central Bank, CADE (antitrust), IPEA
- **99 interactive JavaScript graphs** (JSXGraph, Plotly, D3.js)
- **ANPEC exam preparation**: 72 real exam questions from Brazil's graduate economics entrance exam
- **Python computational notebooks**: 6 notebooks for simulation and visualization
- **AI-integrated exercises**: 9 exercises using LLMs as pedagogical tools
- **Instructor's guide**: Suggested exams, 30-session teaching plan, pedagogical tips
