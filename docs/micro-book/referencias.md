# Referências Bibliográficas

## Manuais de Microeconomia

- Besanko, David, e Ronald Braeutigam. 2014. [*Microeconomics*](https://books.google.com.br/books?id=BeoengEACAAJ). 5ª ed. Hoboken: Wiley.
- Browning, Edgar K., e Mark A. Zupan. 2014. [*Microeconomics: Theory and Applications*](https://books.google.com.br/books?id=Q_9EBQAAQBAJ). 12ª ed. Hoboken: Wiley.
- Goolsbee, Austan, Steven Levitt, e Chad Syverson. 2020. [*Microeconomics*](https://books.google.com.br/books?id=RgzODwAAQBAJ). 3ª ed. New York: Worth Publishers.
- Mas-Colell, Andreu, Michael D. Whinston, e Jerry R. Green. 1995. [*Microeconomic Theory*](https://books.google.com.br/books?id=KGtegVzqhMkC). New York: Oxford University Press.
- Nicholson, Walter, e Christopher Snyder. 2017. [*Microeconomic Theory: Basic Principles and Extensions*](https://books.google.com.br/books?id=we1LEAAAQBAJ). 12ª ed. Mason: Cengage Learning.
- Perloff, Jeffrey M. 2017. [*Microeconomics: Theory and Applications with Calculus*](https://books.google.com.br/books?id=jGs4EAAAQBAJ). 4ª ed. Boston: Pearson.
- Pindyck, Robert S., e Daniel L. Rubinfeld. 2013. [*Microeconomia*](https://books.google.com.br/books?id=bWveDwAAQBAJ). 8ª ed. São Paulo: Pearson.
- Varian, Hal R. 1992. [*Microeconomic Analysis*](https://books.google.com.br/books?id=m20iQAAACAAJ). 3ª ed. New York: W. W. Norton.
- Varian, Hal R. 2015. [*Microeconomia: Uma Abordagem Moderna*](https://books.google.com.br/books?id=X6s3DwAAQBAJ). 9ª ed. Rio de Janeiro: Elsevier.
