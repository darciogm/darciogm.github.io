# Darcio Genicolo-Martins

**Assistant Professor of Economics**
<br>Insper Institute of Education and Research, Sao Paulo, Brazil

</div>
</div>

[Darcio Genicolo-Martins](https://darciogm.github.io/) is an Assistant Professor of Economics at [Insper Institute of Education and Research](https://www.insper.edu.br/en/) (Sao Paulo, Brazil). His research focuses on [applied microeconomics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Applied_microeconomics), [public economics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Public_economics), and [law & economics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Law_and_economics), particularly on the efficiency and governance of public procurement and auditing processes. He uses [econometrics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Econometrics), [machine learning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_learning), and [NLP](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Natural_language_processing) to evaluate and improve institutional performance.

Darcio is affiliated with Insper's [Center for Regulation and Democracy (CRD)](https://www.insper.edu.br/centros-e-institutos/centro-regulacao-e-democracia/) and collaborates with the [Becker Friedman Institute (University of Chicago)](https://bfi.uchicago.edu/) and the [Center for Law and Economics at ETH Zurich](https://cce.ethz.ch/).
