🤔 Something Weird Is Going On
Among 41,000 firms in São Paulo, nearly 17,000 never won a single tender. Some keep coming back — again, and again, and again. One firm bid on 97 contracts over ten years. Won zero. That’s not bad luck. That’s a job description.
🎭 The Cover Bidders
Brazilian law often requires at least 3 bidders. Cartels need extras to fill the room. Cover bidders submit absurdly high bids, lose on purpose, and collect a side payment. They’re not competitors. They’re extras in a rigged play.
