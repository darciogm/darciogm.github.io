# Referências Bibliográficas

## Manuais de Microeconomia

- BESANKO, D.; BRAEUTIGAM, R. *Microeconomics*. 5. ed. Hoboken: Wiley, 2014.
- BROWNING, E. K.; ZUPAN, M. A. *Microeconomics: Theory and Applications*. 12. ed. Hoboken: Wiley, 2014.
- GOOLSBEE, A.; LEVITT, S.; SYVERSON, C. *Microeconomics*. 3. ed. New York: Worth Publishers, 2020.
- MAS-COLELL, A.; WHINSTON, M. D.; GREEN, J. R. *Microeconomic Theory*. New York: Oxford University Press, 1995.
- NICHOLSON, W.; SNYDER, C. *Microeconomic Theory: Basic Principles and Extensions*. 12. ed. Mason: Cengage Learning, 2017.
- PERLOFF, J. M. *Microeconomics: Theory and Applications with Calculus*. 4. ed. Boston: Pearson, 2017.
- PINDYCK, R. S.; RUBINFELD, D. L. *Microeconomia*. 8. ed. São Paulo: Pearson, 2013.
- VARIAN, H. R. *Microeconomia: Uma Abordagem Moderna*. 9. ed. Rio de Janeiro: Elsevier, 2015.
- VARIAN, H. R. *Microeconomic Analysis*. 3. ed. New York: W. W. Norton, 1992.
