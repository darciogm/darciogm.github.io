    Darcio Genicolo-Martins¶

    Assistant Professor of Economics
    Insper Institute of Education and Research, Sao Paulo, Brazil

    Darcio Genicolo-Martins is an Assistant Professor of Economics at Insper Institute of Education and Research (Sao Paulo, Brazil). His research focuses on applied microeconomics, public economics, and law & economics, particularly on the efficiency and governance of public procurement and auditing processes. He uses econometrics, machine learning, and NLP to evaluate and improve institutional performance.

    Darcio is affiliated with Insper's Center for Regulation and Democracy (CRD) and collaborates with the Becker Friedman Institute (University of Chicago) and the Center for Law and Economics at ETH Zurich.
